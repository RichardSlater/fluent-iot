Fluent IoT

A demonstration of how technology can react to events.

About Fluent IoT

I wanted to build something for my daughter; not simply to solve a real problem for her, but also to show her that there is a process in solving a problem.

At first I built something that allowed her to control a Philips Hue lamp with simple infra-red remote controll then I asked here what else she would like it to do. It turns out that speech, light and music are very appealing to her and so I built Fluent IoT as a platform to deliver what she wanted to get out of the technology.

I hope that this little project will inspire others to use technology to 'hack' their surroundings to make their homes and offices work well for them at all levels from architecture through to technology

Simulator

It would be quite difficult try out Fluent IoT quickly, you would have to buy devices just to see it in action. Instead I have built a simulator to allow you to try it out inside your browser, the Fluent IoT components are the same it's jut the remote control and the lamp that are simulated.
